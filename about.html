The hospital is very conveniently located on the Mata Road, Near main Gurgaon Bus stand which makes it accessible to patients coming from the neighbouring as well as far flung areas. The Gurgaon Railway station is about 5 kms from the hospital and public transport from all parts of NCR is available to reach the hospital. The hospital has 50 inpatient beds which includes Single Private and Shared Accomodation. The rooms have the provision of natural light and airconditioning to provide maximum comfort to the patients during their hospital stay.

Lotus hospital started operations in the year 2008. In the last 5 years the hospital has emerged as a major referral centre for high end surgeries and advanced critical care. The hospital specializes in all kinds of Gastrointestinal, Gynaecological, Orthopaedic, Urological, Neurosurgical, Cancer and Reconstructive surgeries. The hospital is jointly promoted by Laparoscopic and General Surgeon Dr. Umesh Gupta and Gynaecologist & Obstetrician Dr. Ruchi Gupta.
